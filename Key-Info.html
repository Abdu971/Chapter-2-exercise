Key Information

Key Info: Jack Reacher Series

  • Author: Lee Child (books 1–24), Andrew Child (from book 25 onward).
  • Genre: Thriller, Crime, Mystery, Action.
  • Main Character: Jack Reacher – a former U.S. Army Military Police officer turned drifter and vigilante investigator.
  • Recurring Themes: Justice, lone-wolf hero, military background, detective work, hand-to-hand combat, strategic thinking.
  • Movies: Jack Reacher (2012) and Jack Reacher: Never Go Back (2016) starring Tom Cruise.
  • TV Series: Reacher (2022–present) starring Alan Ritchson (Prime Video).
